About BBL Public School

Late Krishna Kumar Agarwal

Our respected founder, Late Krishna Kumar Agarwal, envisioned a school that nurtures minds, builds character, and inspires a lifelong love of learning.

Late Brij Bhushan Lal Agarwal

The Late Shri Brij Bhushan Lal Agarwal, the father of the founder of the school was an enlightened and public spirited citizen of Bareilly. He left for heavenly abode long before the inception of school but his presence is always felt as a guiding star.

Our Journey

Established in 1990, BBL Public School began as a small institution with a vision to provide quality education in Bareilly. Over the years, it has grown into a network of schools with a reputation for excellence in academics, sports, and cultural activities.
