Overview & History

  • Established in 1990 by Sh. Krishna Kumar Agarwal, in memory of his father under the B.B.L. Charitable Trust, with co-founder Sh. Sarvesh Kumar Agarwal

  • Initially located on Alakhnath Road, with additional branches on Pilibhit Road (2008) and Deen Dayal Puram (2002)

  • The school is affiliated with CBSE and provides Nursery to Class XII education

  • Focuses on blending traditional Indian values with a modern, technology-driven learning approach

INFRASTRUCTURE & FACILITIES

  • Buildings: Main blocks designed for safety and good lighting, earthquake-resistant .

  • Labs: Includes separate Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Maths, Composite, and two Computer Science labs

  • Library: Large collection of books, journals, magazines, periodicals, and newspapers (nearly 2,932 books and 1,610 periodicals) .
